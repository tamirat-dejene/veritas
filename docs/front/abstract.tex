\begin{abstract}
\itshape
The rapid growth of digital education and remote recruitment has increased the demand for reliable, scalable, and secure online assessment systems. Traditional examination and evaluation methods often suffer from limitations such as lack of flexibility, poor scalability, manual administration overhead, and challenges in ensuring fairness and identity verification. This report addresses these challenges by designing and documenting a comprehensive enterprise-level online assessment management system that supports multiple stakeholders, including system administrators, enterprise administrators, staff members, candidates, and external payment services.

The proposed system provides a structured and role-based platform for managing the complete lifecycle of digital assessments. Key functionalities include enterprise registration and subscription management, staff and candidate management, exam creation and scheduling, candidate assignment, secure exam delivery, identity verification, automated result processing, reporting, and certificate issuance. The system also integrates dashboard-based analytics to support informed decision-making at both system-wide and enterprise-specific levels. A modular use-case-driven design approach is adopted to clearly define system behavior, interactions, and responsibilities across different actors.

The findings of this work demonstrate that a well-defined use case specification significantly improves system clarity, maintainability, and scalability. By separating responsibilities among actors and enforcing role-based access control, the system enhances security and operational efficiency. Features such as automated reporting, real-time dashboards, and payment processing further reduce administrative effort while increasing transparency and usability. Additionally, identity verification and monitoring mechanisms contribute to the integrity and credibility of online examinations.

The significance of this work lies in its practical applicability to modern e-learning platforms, corporate recruitment processes, and professional certification systems. The proposed design can serve as a foundation for implementing robust online assessment solutions that align with current technological and organizational requirements. Overall, this report contributes to the field of software engineering by presenting a structured, extensible, and enterprise-ready assessment system design that addresses real-world challenges in digital evaluation environments.
\end{abstract}
